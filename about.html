About Us

Who are we?

Kala Nirmanam is a social enterprise founded in 2021. We collaborate with the Nakashi Art community to bring to you these beautiful, authentic cheriyal scroll paintings on home d√©cor products. What do we do?

At Kalanirmanam, we work with Nakashi artisans to create products like tote bags, tea coasters, table mats and much more, and display their original designs on them. A portion of our proceeds goes back to the community, so that the legacy of Nakashi Artisans can live on. We have travelled into the interiors of South West India to identify products that stand out for their combination of tradition, craft and originality. We provide value to customers and product manufacturers. Our product manufacturers are typically individuals who operate in remote villages. Each item chosen by you carries a rich history. We use a combination of technology and traditional methods, along with the best management practices and customer service, so the customers receive the best quality products.

Why do we do this? The endeavor is to source from individuals who belong to the weaker sections of society. Often, the artisan is not accorded value for their creations as their work goes through a chain of distributors. We wish to also showcase the product creators for their work. Also, as Cheriyal Scroll Painting is a dying art, our objective is to help the artists promote this art form.
